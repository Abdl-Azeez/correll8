About C8

We come from the Oil & Gas, Ports & Logistics, and Banking & Insurance industries. From our Singaporean corporate center, we draw on those cross-industry vertical experiences delivering transformational organisation consultancy, and digitally-enabled solutions to our global clients.

Having experienced the transformation, first-hand as ‘the client’ we know how the ideal journey should look and feel. We give you, our clients the benefit of that experience.

Our cross-industry subject matter experts engage with our clients. Together, we discuss the strategic and economic benefits associated with business and digital transformation, we uncover client aspirations and pain points, then apply that knowledge to design and implement a solution.
